ABOUT THE COLLEGE

SRM TRP Engineering College is named after this well-known educationist and philanthropist Dr.T.R.Paarivendhar. The institution continues the blazing glory in the field of education, deservedly bequeathing it from SRM Group of Institutions. It is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education and affiliated to Anna University, Chennai. The College is located on NH-45 at Irungalur near Samayapuram, famous for its Mariamman temple and at a distance of about 25kms from Tiruchirappalli, a city well known for its education, industrial development and connectivity with all parts of the state. The College is constructed in a rural area hence it is a quiet and pollution-free campus.

About the Department

Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering started in the year 2010 with intake of 60 students and additional intake of 120 students in the year of 2012. The department faculty members are well experienced and technically sound imparting knowledge and skills in responsibilities and leadership.

The Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering is one among the core courses offered in our college. The department strongly believes in working towards a goal which is not merely theoretical in content but practical approach. It has separate department library with more than 1500 books. Highly qualified and experienced faculty with specialization in Power Systems, Power Electronics and Applied Electronics.

The Department of computer science and engineering (CSE) is established in the year 2010 to meet the requirements of the emerging IT industries. The department has well equipped computing laboratories, a rich repository of software covering, and a wide spectrum of applications. It has a strong commitment for developing competent programmers, both class room sessions and hands on sessions, given to students.

About The Conference

International Conference on Artificial Intelligence for IoT and Power Grid Networks aims at bringing together for leading academic scientists, researchers and scholars to exchange their research experience, the most recent innovations and concerns as well. It assists the professionals to be engaged to maintain the integrity and competence of the profession.

Programme Objective

The aim of ICAIIPGN’21 is to provide an opportunity for students, research scholars, academicians and industry professionals for sharing and exploring new areas of research and development and to discuss emerging issues and novel methods. It will provide a gateway to enhance their knowledge in the area of Internet of Things, Artificial Intelligence, Power Grid and other related fields.

Call for Papers

Authors are invited to contribute the conference by submitting novel unpublished articles in the following areas but not restricted to:

Paper Format: The full length of paper adhered to the following rules for paper submission.

  • Paper size: A4
  • Font Type & Size: Times New Roman & 12
  • Line Space: Double

Note: Once your paper is accepted for the conference, you have to submit Final version of your paper.

Publication: All papers will go through under peer review process being accepted for the publication. All the accepted papers for the conference will be published in SCOPUS indexed Journal and Book Chapter series.

  1. Advances in Parallel Computing - IOS Press
  2. Journal of Physics: Conference Series - IOP science
  3. Communications in Computer and Information Science- Springer Book series

Registration

Registration is mandatory for at least one author of an accepted paper. The selected papers should be appeared in the proceedings, technical presentation and included in the journal publication.
